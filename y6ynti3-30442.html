    1. What is a title loan?
    A title loan is a type of secured loan where borrowers use their vehicle’s title as collateral to secure the loan amount.

    2. How do title loans work?
    To obtain a title loan, borrowers must provide their vehicle’s title to the lender. The lender assesses the value of the vehicle and offers a loan amount based on that value. The borrower can continue using the vehicle during the loan period.
