    2. Farm Aid:
    Willie Nelson co-founded Farm Aid, an organization that aims to raise awareness and provide financial support to family farmers in the United States. Since its inception in 1985, Farm Aid has been instrumental in providing assistance to struggling farmers, hosting benefit concerts, and advocating for sustainable agricultural practices.
