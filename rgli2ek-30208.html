    2. Founding Wendy’s:
    In 1969, Dave Thomas founded Wendy’s, a fast-food chain that would become one of the largest hamburger franchises worldwide. Wendy’s unique approach to serving fresh, made-to-order food quickly gained popularity and allowed Thomas to expand the brand exponentially.
