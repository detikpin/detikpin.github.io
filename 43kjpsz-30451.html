    1. The Walking Dead’s Phenomenal Success:
    The Walking Dead, a post-apocalyptic comic book series created by Kirkman, became an instant sensation upon its release in 2003. The Walking Dead franchise has undoubtedly played a significant role in skyrocketing Kirkman’s net worth.

Kirkman’s talent for storytelling and his ability to create complex and relatable characters have earned him a devoted fan base.

    3. Skybound Entertainment:
