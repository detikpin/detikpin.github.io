    1. What is a payday loan?
    A payday loan is a short-term loan designed to provide quick cash to individuals facing unexpected financial emergencies. These loans are typically repaid on the borrower’s next payday.

    Yes, many lenders in West Hattiesburg offer online payday loan options, making it convenient for borrowers to apply and receive funds without leaving their homes.
