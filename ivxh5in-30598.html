    5. Liability vs. Full Coverage: When selecting an auto insurance policy, it’s important to understand the difference between liability and full coverage. Liability insurance covers damages and injuries caused to others in an accident for which you are responsible. Full coverage, on the other hand, includes liability coverage and also protects your vehicle against damage and theft. While full coverage offers more comprehensive protection, it is typically more expensive than liability coverage alone.
