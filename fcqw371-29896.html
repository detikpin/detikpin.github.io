    Tiger Woods Range Routine: Mastering the Art of Consistency

    When it comes to golf, Tiger Woods is a name that needs no introduction. Known for his exceptional skills and numerous achievements in the sport, Woods has become an icon in the golfing world. One aspect that has contributed to his success is his rigorous range routine. In this article, we will delve into the details of Tiger Woods’ range routine and explore five interesting facts about his training methods.

    Tiger Woods’ range routine is a meticulous and disciplined practice that has helped him maintain consistency and precision in his game. Here are five intriguing facts about his routine:

    1. Early Morning Start: Woods prefers to begin his range session before the sun rises. He believes that the tranquility of the early morning hours helps him focus better. By starting his practice before most golfers have even woken up, he gains a sense of exclusivity and calmness that aids his concentration.

    2. Warm-Up Exercises: Before hitting the range, Woods engages in a series of warm-up exercises to prepare his body for the physical demands of the game. These exercises include stretching, light jogging, and swinging weighted clubs. This routine helps improve his flexibility, agility, and overall performance on the course.

    3. Focused Drills: Woods is known for his precision and accuracy, and his range routine reflects this. He spends a significant amount of time practicing specific shots and drills that target different aspects of his game. From working on iron shots to perfecting his driving, each drill is carefully designed to enhance a specific skill set.
