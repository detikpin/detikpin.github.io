    1. What factors affect auto insurance rates in Statesville, NC?
    Auto insurance rates are influenced by factors such as your driving record, age, vehicle type, coverage options, and credit score.

    Full coverage insurance typically costs more than liability-only coverage. However, rates can vary depending on the insurer and individual circumstances.

    Yes, many insurance companies offer safe driver discounts for maintaining a clean driving record over a certain period.

    While it’s not legally required, having uninsured/underinsured motorist coverage protects you if you’re involved in an accident with a driver who lacks sufficient insurance.
