    2. Liability vs. Full Coverage:
    When it comes to auto insurance, you have two primary options: liability and full coverage. Liability insurance covers damages to another person’s vehicle or property if you are at fault in an accident. Full coverage insurance, on the other hand, provides liability coverage as well as protection for your vehicle against theft, vandalism, or other damages. While liability insurance is generally cheaper, full coverage offers more comprehensive protection.

    3. Discounts for Safe Drivers:
    In Lauderdale Lakes, insurance companies often provide discounts for safe driving habits. If you have a clean driving record with no accidents or traffic violations, you may be eligible for lower rates.     Florida law requires all drivers to carry auto insurance.
