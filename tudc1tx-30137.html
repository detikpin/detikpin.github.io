    Common Questions about Title Loans in Simpsonville, SC:

    1. What is a title loan?
    A title loan is a type of secured loan where the borrower uses their vehicle’s title as collateral to secure the loan. The lender holds onto the title until the loan is repaid in full.

    2. How do title loans work?
    To obtain a title loan, you must own a vehicle with a clear title. The lender evaluates the value of your vehicle and offers a loan based on a percentage of its worth. Once the loan is approved, you hand over the title to the lender and receive the funds.

    3. Can I still drive my car with a title loan?
    Yes, you can continue driving your car while repaying the title loan. The lender holds onto the title but does not take physical possession of the vehicle.

    4. What happens if I fail to repay the title loan?
    If you default on the loan, the lender has the right to repossess your vehicle. However, they will typically work with you to find a solution before resorting to repossession.

    5. Does my credit score affect my eligibility for a title loan?
    No, title loans do not require a credit check. As long as you own a vehicle with a clear title, you can be eligible for a title loan.
