    Interesting Facts about Todd Rundgren

    1. Musical Prodigy Turned Producer
    Before embarking on his solo career, Todd Rundgren showcased his musical prowess as a teenager. He played in various bands and even formed his own group, The Nazz.     Rundgren has always been at the forefront of music technology. In the late 1970s, he was one of the pioneers in experimenting with music videos. His video for the song “Time Heals” became the first music video to ever air on MTV. Rundgren’s interest in technology continued to evolve, leading him to explore virtual reality and online concert experiences, long before they became mainstream.
